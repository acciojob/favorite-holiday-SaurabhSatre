Pune, Maharashtra


Pune is a vibrant city blending rich history with modern development, known as the "Oxford of the East" for its educational institutions and the "Cultural Capital of Maharashtra."

  1. Shaniwar Wada - Historic 18th-century fort palace
  2. Sinhagad Fort - Ancient hill fortress with panoramic views
  3. Aga Khan Palace - National monument of Gandhi's imprisonment
  4. Dagdusheth Temple - Famous Ganpati temple
  5. Khadakwasla Dam - Scenic lakeside retreat
  • Misal Pav - Spicy sprouted curry with bread
  • Bakarwadi - Crispy sweet-spicy snack
  • Pithla-Bhakri - Traditional rural meal
  • Puran Poli - Sweet festive bread
  • Vada Pav - Iconic street burger
